MOBILE LEGENDS

Gameplay. Mobile Legends: Bang Bang is a multiplayer online battle arena (MOBA) game designed for mobile phones. The game is free-to-play and is only monetized through in-game purchases like characters and skins. Each player can control a selectable character, called a Hero, with unique abilities and traits.

5 reasons why this game is my favorite:

  • You can play with other people.
  • It's a fun game.
  • Graphics are nice and no bugs are shown.
  • It allows you to think in battle and how you could win.
  • It allows you to strategize and enhance teamwork.
